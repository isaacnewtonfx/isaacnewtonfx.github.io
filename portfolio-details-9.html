Real Estate Sales Map

An interactive Web GIS Application for advertising real-estate assets to potential clients.

  • Completed: 2019
  • Context: Where Geospatial
  • Tools: Django, ReactJS, TravisCI, PostgreSQL/PostGIS, Leaflet
  • Try it: https://estateman.newtongis.com

The Problem

Most Real Estate companies are not aware of how GIS could be used to effectively manage their properties as well as marketing to potential clients who visit their websites. They normally show pictures of houses and apartments but they could do better with a Geographic Information System.

The Solution

I developed a solution to allow Real Estate Companies to manage, visualize and market their properties to potential clients who visits their website so that clients can send them emails for enquiry on any interested property and discuss purchasing plans.
